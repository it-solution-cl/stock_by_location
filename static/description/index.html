Warehouse Stocks By Locations Odoo Apps

This Odoo apps support product variant and product template warehouse location in Odoo if you get confused to see quantity of product for each location in your warehouse then,this app is really helpful for you to see stock by location for products and stock by location reports for products. This odoo module will help you to see stock of each location on product level, you just need to open product-template/product-variant view, to see stock of all location of your warehouse, This application satisfies the desire to get all the states of stock inventory quantities of particular product in a graphic by product's all internal locations and you can also print those reports for stock balance of each warehouse and warehouse locations.

Features

Stock Report in Graphic View.

One graphic view of all the states is given on the product variant and the product template form where each line indicates internal location.

Gives Calculated Quantity.

This quantities are derived by calculating all the quants and moves of the product.

Gives View for On Hand Quantity.

You can have the idea of On Hand Quantity, Incoming ,Outgoing Quantity and Forecasted Quantity at glance on the table shown in the image here.
